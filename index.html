I'm Alper Şahıstan

A Master's student and teaching asistant at Bilkent University Department of Computer Science. My areas of research include ray-tracing, volume-rendering, visualization and computer graphics. I am also interested in HPC and computational geometry.
Research experience
  • I'm currently remotely collaborating with Dr.Ingo Wald, Nate Morricial, and Stefan Zellman on a 3D scientific visualization paper. Research is focused on compaction and hardware-assisted efficient traversal of unstructured meshes over multiple nodes.
  • I also utilized compact tetrahedral-meshes as acceleration structures for ray-tracing in a previous government-funded(TÜBİTAK) study supervised by Prof. Güdükbay Of Bilkent University.
